Welcome to Project Zoom

About Us

Project Zoom is a comprehensive management system designed specifically for construction sites. It aims to improve site management efficiency, transparency, and collaboration through digital means. Our system integrates multiple functions such as work task allocation, progress tracking, defect management, document management, safety training, and financial management.

Our mission is to simplify work processes, enhance project management capabilities, and reduce delays caused by information asymmetry. We strive to optimize resource allocation, improve project quality, and strengthen safety management.

Our Products

Our system supports international multilingual capabilities, breaking down language and cultural barriers to provide a full range of management solutions for global construction projects. Key features include:

  • Task and Document Management
  • Resource Optimization
  • Safety and Quality Management
  • Financial Process Simplification
  • Intelligent Report Generation

We offer cross-platform support to ensure a seamless working experience on different devices, and our intelligent report generation adapts to different countries' reporting formats and requirements.
